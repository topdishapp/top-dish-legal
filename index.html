Top Dish — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

Top Dish (“we,” “us”) helps you find the best dish at restaurants near you. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We keep data to the minimum the app needs to work. We do not sell your data, we do not use it for advertising, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Information we collect

When you create an account (optional — you can browse without one). If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name, email address, and a user identifier from that provider. With Sign in with Apple you may choose to hide your email; that’s fully supported.

Anonymous session. When you first open the app we create an anonymous session identified by a random ID. It contains no personal information and lets the app work before — or without — you signing in.

Content you create. Restaurants you save, your checklist progress, and any dish votes or notes you add are stored with your account so they sync across your devices.

Profile photo (optional). If you set a profile photo, we store the image you choose.

Location (used on your device only). If you grant location permission, your location is used on your device to sort nearby restaurants and center the map. Your location is never sent to or stored on our servers.

We do not collect your contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers, and the app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

How we use information

  • To provide core features (showing dishes, saving spots, checklists, notes).
  • To sign you in and sync your data across your devices.
  • To maintain anonymous, aggregate counts (for example, how many people liked a dish). These aggregates are not tied to your identity in the app.

Service providers

We use a small number of providers strictly to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication and storage of your account and profile photo.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — hosting our content API and your saved/checklist/notes data.

These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account at any time in the app: open your profile (avatar, top-right) → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account and associated data — saved spots, checklist progress, votes, and notes — from our systems and removes your authentication record. This cannot be undone.

Children

Top Dish is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with our service providers under their security controls.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective date” above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
